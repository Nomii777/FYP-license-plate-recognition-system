How It Works
  1. Upload a video containing vehicles with visible license plates
  2. Our system will process multiple frames from the video
  3. License plates will be detected in each frame
  4. Detected plates will be processed for text recognition
  5. Results will be displayed with the video and extracted text
For best results, use videos with clear views of license plates and good lighting conditions.
